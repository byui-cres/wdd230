White Space and Clean Design

owllabs.com

This website use some space and limited elements certain arts of the page and group them together to create a minimal design. It gives the users more focus on what is showing up. There will be no destruction to focus on the main content.

The page look clean and appealing. This will cause people to stay more to page and even click some links to know more of the product. When simplicity are properly utilized, it can lead to more quality engagement and user experience.
