Purpose:

The purpose of this website is to give its members the most important information to promote the business interest of the members of the organization, give people a quick overview of towns businesses and its operating hours, highlight the events sponsored by the chamber and show off the members of the chamber and their businesses.

It is also the purpose of the website to invite non-members to join the chamber and help the organization grow significantly.
